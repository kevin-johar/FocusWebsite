Focus

Problem Definition

Utilizing existing EEG technology to routinely profile and update individuals on brain activity as an indicator of stressors and neural health.

Focus App

With the help of the Muse Headband, EEG data is collected from the user and they are challenged to relax their mind to change the game elements from randomized colours to a single calming colour.

Application

  • Monitoring athlete's brains
  • Physiotheraphy to train the brain
  • Creating an active database to improve health system and treatment methods
  • Biometric analysis

Future Improvements

  • Improved accuracy
  • Physiotheraphy to train the brain
  • Providing useful data for targeted marketing
  • Proactively understanding a client's mental health
